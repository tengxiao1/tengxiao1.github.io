Teng Xiao

I am a postdoctoral researcher (and Young Investigator) at the Allen Institute for AI （AI2） and the University of Washington, advised by Prof. Noah A. Smith and Prof. Hanna Hajishirzi. I completed my PhD at Pennsylvania State University, where I am advised by Prof. Vasant Honavar. I am interested in machine learning, reinforcement learning, language models. Specifically, I am currently working on: (i) AI Alignment with Human Values and Preferences. (ii) Reasoning and Planning for Autonomous Decision Making.
